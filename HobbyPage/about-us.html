hi! i like to cook, write, play music, and take photos. i recently moved from brooklyn to a farm on the north dakota-minnesota border, where my husband is a fifth-generation farmer. the farm life is pretty swell: i get to bake all day with wheat from our fields, hang out with my little flock of chickens, and hunker down for some very extreme (but cozy!) winters. i graduated from juilliard with a degree in percussion but during my time in new york, i fell wildly in love with food. i currently spend most of my time playing with food but still play music occasionally!
